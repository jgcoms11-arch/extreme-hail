We Fix Every Dent — No Matter the Storm

Hail damage can range from a few small dimples to hundreds of dents across every panel. We assess the full extent of the damage and use the right repair method for each situation — paintless dent repair (PDR) when possible, and traditional body repair when the damage requires it.

Our team works carefully through every affected panel to restore your vehicle to its pre-storm condition. We document everything clearly for your estimate.

Why Fix Hail Damage

Don't Let Hail Damage Sit

Hail damage affects more than just appearance — it can reduce your vehicle's resale value and worsen over time if left unaddressed.

Protect Resale Value

Hail dents visibly reduce your vehicle's value. Getting it repaired restores both appearance and market value.

Prevent Paint Damage

Cracked or chipped paint from hail impacts can lead to rust and further deterioration if not addressed promptly.
