Hail & Collision Repair

Honest answers to what customers ask us most before scheduling a repair or estimate.

Yes — hail damage is one of our specialties. We handle everything from minor dimpling to severe storm damage across all panels. We use paintless dent repair (PDR) where the paint is intact, and traditional body repair methods where more significant damage requires it.

We can help you understand the repair estimate, explain what damage was found, and let you know what photos and documentation are typically useful to have. We are not insurance agents and do not process claims — for questions about your specific coverage, contact your insurance provider directly.

It depends on the severity of the damage. Minor hail damage with PDR can sometimes be completed in a day or two. More severe damage involving multiple panels or paint work can take longer. We'll give you a realistic timeline as part of your free estimate.

Yes. We handle full collision repair including frame straightening, panel replacement, bumper repair, and paint refinishing. Whether it's a minor fender bender or more significant structural damage, we'll assess it honestly and give you a clear plan.
