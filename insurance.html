How We Help

Understanding the Estimate & Repair Process

We don't process claims or act as your insurance agent — but we do help you understand what's needed and what the repair involves at every step.

What We Can Help You With

1

Assess Your Damage

We thoroughly inspect your vehicle and document all damage — so you have a clear picture of what needs to be repaired and why.

2

Provide a Detailed Estimate

We give you a written estimate that clearly breaks down the repair scope, parts, and labor — in plain language you can actually read.

3

Explain What Photos to Take

We'll tell you what damage photos are typically useful for documentation purposes so you have what you need on hand.

4

Answer Your Questions

Have questions about the repair process, timeline, or what's covered? We'll answer honestly and help you understand your options.

What to Gather Before Calling

Photos of all visible damage (all angles)
Your vehicle year, make, and model
Description of how the damage occurred
Any existing repair history on the vehicle

We help customers understand their estimate and the repair process. We are not insurance agents or adjusters and do not provide legal or insurance advice. For questions about your specific coverage, contact your insurance provider directly.
